Refund & Cancellation Policy

We ensure that every product is carefully packed and shipped. Since we deal in jewellery, returns and refunds are allowed only in the following cases:

  • If you receive a wrong product.
  • If the product is damaged during delivery.

In such cases, please notify us within 48 hours of receiving the order along with an unboxing video and pictures. We will initiate a replacement or refund as per your request.

Order cancellations are not allowed once the product has been shipped.
