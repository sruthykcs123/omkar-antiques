Shipping & Delivery Policy

We deliver our jewellery products across India through trusted courier partners.

  • Orders are usually processed within 2–4 working days after payment confirmation.
  • Delivery timelines vary depending on the location, generally between 5–10 working days.
  • You will receive tracking details once your order is shipped.
  • Shipping charges (if applicable) will be mentioned at checkout.

We take full care in packaging to ensure safe delivery of your jewellery.
